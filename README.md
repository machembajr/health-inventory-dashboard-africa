# Cross-Country Healthcare Supply Chain Analytics Dashboard

A healthcare supply chain analytics project focused on inventory optimization, cold-chain exposure, and therapeutic-area performance across multiple African markets. The project transforms fragmented inventory data into actionable insights for supply chain decision-making.

## Overview

This dashboard analyzes healthcare commodity inventory across Ghana, Kenya, Nigeria, Tanzania, and Uganda. The objective was to identify concentration risks, cold-chain exposure, and product-level dependencies that affect supply chain resilience in African health systems.

The full analysis, including data cleaning, pivot tables, KPI development, and dashboard design, was built end-to-end in Microsoft Excel.

## Key Findings

**Total Inventory Value: $273K+**
Inventory value was concentrated across a few countries and therapeutic areas, highlighting key operational priorities.

**Cold-Chain Dependency: 45 percent ($122.7K)**
Nearly half of all inventory required temperature-controlled storage, creating significant logistics and infrastructure pressure.

**Highest-Value Country: Tanzania at $75.4K**
Tanzania accounted for roughly 28 percent of the total inventory value, indicating both high demand and concentration risk.

**Highest-Value Therapeutic Area: Non-Communicable Diseases at $90.3K**
NCD products exceeded HIV and Malaria inventory value, reflecting the region's changing healthcare needs.

**Top High-Value Products**

- Insulin Human — $54.8K
- Salbutamol Inhaler — $34.3K
- Yellow Fever Vaccine — $23.0K
- Pentavalent Vaccine — $22.9K

**Countries with the Highest Cold-Chain Exposure**

1. Tanzania — $46.8K
2. Nigeria — $16.0K
3. Ghana — $14.7K

## Recommendations

- Strengthen cold-chain infrastructure in high-exposure markets.
- Improve forecasting for NCD and vaccine products.
- Introduce real-time inventory monitoring across distribution points.
- Rebalance slow-moving stock across countries to reduce holding costs.
- Reduce dependency on high-value single products through diversified sourcing.

## Technical Work

- Data cleaning and harmonization across multi-country inventory records.
- Inventory value analysis by country, therapeutic area, and product.
- KPI development for supply chain monitoring.
- Supply chain analytics covering cold-chain and concentration risk.
- Dashboard design and visualization in Microsoft Excel.
